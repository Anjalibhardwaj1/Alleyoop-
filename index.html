About Alleyoop

Alleyoop is a reusable packaging service that offers the ability to deliver goods to consumers by in either a box or mailer format using a convertible container for greater reduction in carbon footprint and ease of use for the customer .

Environmental Impact

While recycling is good for the environment, it has its limits. Single-use packaging such as cardboard boxes and bubble wrap are often not recycled properly. In fact, the EPA found that 30.47 million tons of packaging ended up in landfills in 2018. Recyc-Québec reported that 21% of the household waste is actually constituted of recyclable material. The idea is to make packaging that is durable such that it can be reused multiple times, cutting down on the need for single-use packages that are currently the norm in the shipping industry.
